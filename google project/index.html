About GDG

Google Developer Group Ashgabat invites you to DevFest 2022! DevFest is a local multi-topic tech conference organized by Google Developer Groups (GDGs) around the world. The goal is to rally the community, to bring the technology closer to both developers and the business sector, where there is an opportunity to meet experts in their field, how to share experience and learn new things, share knowledge for the benefit of common development! During such events, where people of similar interests converge, new ideas, start-ups are born, and, as a rule, the technology sector as a whole develops.The target audience of the event is both students and professionals or all those who are interested in technical specialties and who are not indifferent to influencing the progress of the local community. No matter what Google technology you use, you are invited to DevFest to improve your skills and connect with the local community!
